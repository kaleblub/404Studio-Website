About Balanced Accountant

At Balanced Accountant, we understand that managing finances can be overwhelming for small business owners. With nearly two decades of experience across industries like property management, construction, engineering, and even entertainment, we’ve seen firsthand the financial challenges businesses face.
Our foundation is built on expertise, with degrees in Accounting and Finance from Kansas State University and Tennessee Technological University. But beyond numbers, our real strength lies in practical experience—working in industries such as legal, non-profit, and education, handling daily accounting, payroll, collections, and financial strategy.
What sets us apart is our client-focused approach. We believe that every business has a unique story, and our goal is to be your financial guide—helping you navigate challenges, maximize growth, and create a stable foundation for the future. Whether you're looking for bookkeeping support, tax planning, or strategic financial advice, we’re here to partner with you on your journey to success.

  • What tax deductions and credits am I missing?
  • How do I create a budget that actually works for my business?
  • When should I hire an accountant versus doing it myself?
  • How can I make my business more attractive to lenders or investors?

Whether you're a freelancer, startup, or established business, we provide bookkeeping, tax planning, and financial strategy tailored to your needs.
